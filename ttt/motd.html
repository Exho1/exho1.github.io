Exho's TTT server rules

These rules attempt to cover most of the basics of TTT. Please use common sense and don't try to exploit loop holes or vague wording

General rules

1. Have fun and be civil with each other

2. Do not deathmatch, this is not Call of Duty

3. Do not ghost/metagame

4. Do not kill AFKs. Let them be slain or kicked.

5. Do not traitor bait (firing randomly to invoke a reaction)

6. Do not prop kill but you can prop block and prop surf

7. No offensive sprays

8. Do not falsely report players

9. Just because you RDMed a Traitor does not make it not RDM

10. You can kill on reasonable suspicion during overtime

11. Don't goomba stomp people

Role rules

Non-Detective roles may only claim during overtime. You MUST type this in chat

Traitors who die from a fellow traitors C4, its your fault.

Detectives may not grapple abuse (constantly grapple or grapple to unreachable areas)

Traitors must warn their Traitor buddies if they plan to jihad

Detectives cannot order other terrorists to harm themselves (besides using a T tester)

Text rules

Do not advertise your community or product

Do not spam chat with binds

Do not be overly rude, racist, or offensive

Voice rules

Your name must be easily pronouncable

Dont impersonate other players

No disruptive names like "Traitor" or "Nobody"

Your name should not be too similar to other players in the server

Map rules

Turning off the lights is KOS unless the Detective says otherwise. The Detective cannot force the lights off

Glitching the Richland laser trap is bannable

Hindering the ability to achieve an objective by throwing away key items or breaking testers is KOS
